Project Experience

Analysis and optimization of Flow over a Bullet in different medium.
  • The objective of this project work is to perform a flow simulation around a bullet in two different medium at different speeds and then compare and analyze the variation in drag coefficient of the bullet and to obtain the pressure distribution.
  • The bullet used for this project work is 5.56*45 mm caliber NATO round bullet. The selected medium are Air and Water.
  • The bullet design is based upon the Hornady Bullet design.
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA) are performed upon these models using ANSYS Fluent to analyze the flow estimation over bullet body. The flow analysis has been done at speeds ranging from 50-900 m/s.
